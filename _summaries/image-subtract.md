proc: image-subtract
params: "img1 img2 [description]"
docs: |
  "Subtract" `img2` from `img1`, decreasing the opacity of each
  pixel in `img1` by the opacity of the corresponding pixel in
  `img2`. `image-subtract` does not otherwise affect the colors
  in `img1`.
